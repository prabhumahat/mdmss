Mahakavi Devkota Model Secondary School

Mahakavi Devkota Model Secondary School is located in the beautiful city of Sonawal, Nepal. It is one of the leading schools in the region, known for its excellent academic programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive learning environment.

Facilities

The school offers a variety of facilities to its students, including a well-stocked library, modern science and computer labs, spacious classrooms, and a large playground. The school also provides boarding facilities for students who come from far away.

Academic Programs

Mahakavi Devkota Model Secondary School offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers all the major subjects, such as mathematics, science, social studies, English, and Nepali. The school also provides extracurricular activities, including music, sports, and arts, to help students develop their interests and skills.
