Welcome to Mahakavi Devkota Model Sec. School

Located in the heart of Sunwal, Mahakavi Devkota Model Sec. School is a premier educational institution offering quality education to students of all ages. With a strong focus on academic excellence and a commitment to providing a supportive and inclusive learning environment, the school has become a hub of learning and innovation.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide a world-class education that prepares students for success in the 21st century. We strive to cultivate critical thinking skills, foster creativity, and develop strong values in our students so that they may lead fulfilling lives and make positive contributions to society.

Our Facilities

Our school is equipped with modern facilities including spacious classrooms, state-of-the-art science laboratories, a library, and a computer lab. We also have a sports ground and a playground for our students to enjoy physical activity.

Our Staff

Our dedicated staff consists of highly qualified and experienced teachers who are passionate about providing a quality education to our students. With their expertise and commitment to excellence, they help our students reach their full potential and prepare them for success.
